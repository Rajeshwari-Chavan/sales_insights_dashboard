# AtliQ Hardware Sales Insights Dashboard 📊

An enterprise-grade data analytics project that transforms raw retail sales data into actionable business intelligence. Using **SQL** for data extraction and **Power BI** for data modeling and interactive visualization, this dashboard identifies revenue leakages and tracks profitability to guide strategic business decisions.

---

## 💡 The Business Problem & Impact
AtliQ Hardware operates a brick-and-mortar electronics business across multiple regional markets. Without a centralized system, tracking dynamic sales trends and hidden regional losses was a massive challenge. 

This dashboard addresses those gaps and provides a clear roadmap to **increase next-quarter revenue by at least 7%** by focusing on three strategic levers:
1. **Plugging Profit Leaks:** Instantly flagging negative-margin regions (e.g., Kanpur and Bengaluru at -0.3%) to review operational costs.
2. **Optimizing High-Performing Strongholds:** Capitalizing on top-tier markets like Delhi NCR, which dominates with a 48.5% profit share.
3. **Restructuring Client Contracts:** Identifying specific clients (e.g., Electricalslance Stores at -2.0% profit margin) who bring in sales volume but bleed bottom-line profits.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Showcased
* **Database Management:** SQL Server (Data extraction, exploration, and schema analysis)
* **BI Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Modeling:** Star Schema methodology with localized fact and dimension tables
* **Analytics & Metrics:** Advanced DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for complex dynamic measures (Revenue Contribution %, Profit Margin %, etc.)
* **UI/UX Design:** Built with a clean, executive-ready corporate theme prioritizing instant scannability and visual hierarchy.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Architecture & Key Insights

The report is streamlined into two highly interactive, cross-filtered pages designed for quick executive action:

### 1. Key Insights (Sales & Revenue Overview)
* **Core Purpose:** Tracks high-level sales volume, regional distribution, and macro time-series trends.
* **Key Finding:** Identified a critical revenue decline toward mid-2020, dropping to a lowest point of ₹15M, signaling the need for an immediate supply chain or market demand review.
* **Market Leader:** Pinpoints Delhi NCR as the primary revenue engine, contributing a staggering ₹520M out of the ₹985M total revenue.

### 2. Profit Margin & Contribution Analysis
* **Core Purpose:** Shifts focus from raw sales volume to true business profitability.
* **Key Finding:** Features custom conditional data bars that instantly isolate loss-making clients and regions.
* **Actionable Insight:** Highlights that while Electricalslance Stores generates baseline revenue, it runs at a net negative profit margin of -2.0%, providing a data-driven trigger for contract re-negotiations.
